Our Programs & Events

Discover how Anjuman e Saifee Hyderabad serves the community through key initiatives and the annual Saifee Run.

Why Our Programs Matter

At Anjuman e Saifee Hyderabad, we uphold the vision of His Holiness Dr. Syedna Mohammad Burhanuddin (TUS) to foster community welfare, education, and spiritual growth. Our mission is to maintain mosques, madrasas, and community centers while providing essential support—so that every Dawoodi Bohra family can access religious, educational, and charitable services.

Through dedicated programs like Religious Expenses, Community Centers, Deeni Taleem, and Charity & Donations, we ensure that mosques remain operational, children receive quality Islamic education, and families in need receive medical and housing assistance. Additionally, the Saifee Run unites supporters in an annual fundraiser to sustain these initiatives and expand our outreach.

Our Core Programs

  • Religious Expenses: Covering mosque upkeep (utilities, repairs, staff salaries), Niaz meals during Muharram & Ramadan, and daily Faiz ul Mawaid tiffins to every Dawoodi Bohra household.
  • Community Centers (Jamaat Khana): Funding utilities, maintenance, and staffing so that Jamaat Khanas remain welcoming hubs for religious gatherings, cultural events, and educational sessions.
  • Deeni Taleem: Providing comprehensive Islamic education—Qur’an memorization (Hifz), Tajweed, Arabic language, Fiqh, Akhbar, and character development—aiming to produce at least one Hafiz from every household.
  • Charity & Donations: Offering medical assistance, home renovation grants, scholarships, and housing support for underprivileged families, ensuring access to healthcare, education, and secure shelter.

Join the Saifee Run

The Saifee Run is our annual charity marathon that brings together participants of all ages to raise funds for Anjuman’s programs. Each kilometer run supports:

  • Municipal mosque renovations and utility costs.
  • New classroom supplies and learning aids for Deeni Taleem students.
  • Therapy sessions and health camps for vulnerable families.
  • Scholarships for deserving youth pursuing higher Islamic studies.

Every runner’s registration fee and sponsorship donation helps us maintain our community centers, expand educational outreach, and provide essential aid to families in need. Join us in making a difference—run, walk, or volunteer to ensure a brighter future for our Jammat!
