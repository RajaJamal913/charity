About Us

Our Origin

What began in 2007 as “Rahein”—an institute dedicated to providing quality education and therapy for under‑privileged special children, especially girls—grew from a shared vision under the umbrella of a local special‑needs institute. When Rahein closed in 2021 due to funding shortfalls, 30 families lost critical support. In response, five passionate advocates joined forces to continue this mission.

Our Mission & Vision

Full Circle formally launched in September 2021 in Lahore, securing a central space where children receive education alongside physical, sensory, cognitive, and speech therapies. We believe every child deserves the chance to thrive—physically, emotionally, and intellectually—and we strive to empower them to face life’s challenges with confidence.

What We Do

Education & Therapy

From structured lessons to specialized therapies, we deliver holistic support—academic tutoring, physical exercises, sensory activities, and speech sessions—tailored to each child’s needs.

Sports & Fitness

In partnership with RunUp Lahore, we encourage outdoor play and fitness, helping children build strength, coordination, and self‑esteem through fun, supervised activities.

Online Learning

When COVID‑19 risks remain, we pivot to Zoom and WhatsApp to deliver live lessons and therapy, ensuring uninterrupted support for children in high‑risk areas.

Community Outreach

We work closely with families, local clinics, and volunteers to build a network of care—providing health check‑ups, counseling, and family training to sustain progress at home.
